# Ginja

Here is the Ginja repo for the webpage **[ginja.io](https://ginja.io)**.

Ginja is a productivity app that helps you unload your thoughts, organize tasks, and follow through with reminders that adapt to your style, mood, and daily rhythm.

## Blog publishing

The site supports secure webhook-based blog publishing at `https://ginja.io/api/webhook`. Setup, payload format, and curl testing instructions are in [docs/blog-webhook.md](docs/blog-webhook.md).
